Platinia Hotels

The Platinia Hotel is part of the first mixed-function real estate project in Cluj-Napoca, namely Platinia Shopping Center & Luxury Suites. This project is an innovative one, creating a perfect functional combination between 3 types of functions: the residential function, the commercial function and the hotel function. The hotel is located in an area well-known to the city’s inhabitants that is the former Ursus Brewery.

Located close to the Cluj Arena Stadium, near the Multipurpose Hall and near the most important tourist attractions of Cluj-Napoca, the Platinia Hotel, the newest 5-star hotel in Transylvania, offers a premium experience to all its guests, recreating luxury and elegance in a contemporary and modern setting.

The Platinia Hotel offers its guests accommodation in 121 spacious suites, with a surface that ranges between 55 sq . m and up to 320 sq. m for the penthouse located on the 11th floor.

The Platinia Hotel provides exclusive accommodation services, true messengers our unparalleled hospitality.
